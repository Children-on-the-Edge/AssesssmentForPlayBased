HOW TO OPEN THIS APP
====================

Windows:
  Double-click "Start PPAT Tool (Windows).bat"

Mac:
  Double-click "Start PPAT Tool (Mac).command"
  (If your Mac blocks it the first time: right-click the file, choose
  "Open", then click "Open" again on the warning popup. You only need
  to do this once.)

Chromebook:
  Open the "index.html" file's folder in the Files app, right-click
  index.html, and choose "Open with" > a browser (e.g. Chrome).

That's it — the app opens in your browser and works fully offline
from then on. All your saved assessments stay on this device only.

TIP: Once it's open, you can pin the browser tab, or bookmark it, so
it's always one click away.

Need it on more than one device, or want it to feel like a "real"
installed app with its own icon (works especially well on
Chromebook)? Ask whoever set this up for you about the "PWA install"
option described in README.md.
